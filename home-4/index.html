MobileMuster works with the Australian public, businesses, and our members to deliver a free, non-profit mobile phone recycling program that accepts all brands and types of mobile phones, plus their batteries, chargers, and accessories. Since 1998 we have...

Recycled 11.9 Million Items

We've recycled approximately 11.9 million individual handsets, batteries, and chargers.

Recycled 1,323 Tonnes

We've collected, recycled, and saved more than 1,323 tonnes of mobile phone components from landfill.

A 96% Recovery Rate

We recover over 96% of the materials in the mobile phones, batteries, and chargers we collect.

Established 3,500 Drop-off Points

MobileMuster has over 3,500 public drop-off and collection points across Australia.

Did you know?
Over 23 million unwanted mobile phones lay around Australian homes, 5 million of which are unusable… MobileMuster safely and securely recycles old mobiles for free.
