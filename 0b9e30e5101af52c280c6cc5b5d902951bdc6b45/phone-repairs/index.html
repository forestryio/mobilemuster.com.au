Mobile Phone Repairs 

Phone repairs can extend the life of your mobile phone. Make sure you look for an experienced and authorised mobile phone repair centre recommended by your mobile phone manufacturer. Check the repair warranty that came with the mobile phone to understand the terms and conditions.  An authorised repair centre will be able to look at the mobile phone and let you know the options for phone repairs.  

Remember that the best source of information about how to repair your mobile phone is the manufacturer or your mobile carrier. For more information on mobile phone manufacturers and carriers, click here. 

If you decide not to get your phone repaired and upgrade to a new phone, don't forget to recycle your old phone. Mobile phone recycling is made free and convenient through the MobileMuster program.  Simply find a convenient drop-off point near you or print a free reply paid label so you can post in your old phone.

MobileMuster accepts all makes and models of mobile phones, batteries, chargers and accessories. As the official recycling program of the mobile phone industry MobileMuster will recycle your old phone in a safe, secure and ethical way. 

Retail Partners

Retailers offer an easy and convenient way for consumers to recycle their old mobile and accessories. To find your nearest drop off point simply use our  store locator on the right hand side. By entering your post code or suburb name we can provide a list of drop off points in your area.
