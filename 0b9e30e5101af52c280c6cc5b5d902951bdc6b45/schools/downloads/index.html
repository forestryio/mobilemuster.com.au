Phonebloks

Phonebloks - new and sustainable mobile phone technology. The concept of Phoneblok is a phone made of detachable bloks. The bloks are connected to the base which locks everything together into a solid phone. If a blok breaks you can easily replace it; if it's getting old just upgrade.
