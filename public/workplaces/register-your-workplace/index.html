REGISTER YOUR WORKPLACE

Register your workplace as a MobileMuster collection point.

To register your workplace with MobileMuster, just complete the form below. Once you're on board, we'll provide you with a free collection unit, promotional materials and support. We also offer a free pick-up service - you can book a pick-up by calling 1800 249 113.
