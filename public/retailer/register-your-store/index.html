REGISTER YOUR STORE

Want to become a MobileMuster collection point? It's easy.

To register your interest in becoming a collection point, just complete the form below. Once you're registered, we'll provide you with a free collection unit, promotional materials, support, and a free pick-up service. Please notes that all mobiles collected in-store become the property of AMTA and cannot given away or resold. See the terms and conditions for more details.

What can be recycled?

MobileMuster accepts all models and makes of mobile phones. Handsets, batteries, chargers and accessories are all accepted - we even collect dummy mobiles. We won't accept non-mobile electronics, but we're committed to keeping anything mobile phone related out of landfill, and recycling whatever we can.

Any questions? Email mobilemuster@amta.org.au or call 1800 249 113 (option 2).
